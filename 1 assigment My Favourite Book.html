My Favorite Book: "The Great Gatsby"

"The Great Gatsby" is a novel written by F.Scott Fitzgerald and was first published in 1925.It is
widely considered one of the greatest American novels.The story is set duringtheRoaring Twentiesand
explores themes of loves,wealth,and the American Dream.

The novel's main character,Jay Gatsby,is famous for his extragant parties and unrequired love for
Daisy Buchanan.His story ultimately leads to tragic consequences.The book is known for its
symbolismand its critical view of the American Dream.
